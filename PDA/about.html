Warm Greetings to all Dental Professionals!

As the General Chairman of the 110th PDA Annual Convention and Scientific Sessions, it is with pride that I am inviting all of you to attend our Annual Convention on May 13-17, 2019 at SMX Convention Center Manila.

Our theme for this year “ Collaboration Towards Dynamic Professionalism” will address the various challenges that we face both in our professionand in our association. The scientificlectures will showcase the best of both local and international lecturers who will focus on evidence - based topics in conformity to the advanced competency programs eligible for cpd points.

In behalf of the organizing committee, let me welcome you all in taking this opportunity to converge for continuing professional development, reconnect with our colleagues and experience the latest technology in the country’s biggest dental exhibition.

Maraming Salamat po!
